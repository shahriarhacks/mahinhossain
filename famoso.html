Shah Rukh Khan

Shah Rukh Khan, often referred to as the "King of Bollywood," is one of the most iconic and influential figures in Indian cinema. Born on November 2, 1965, in New Delhi, India, Khan's journey to stardom is a quintessential rags-to-riches story.

Salman Khan

Salman Khan is a prominent figure in the Indian film industry, renowned for his charisma, acting prowess, and philanthropy. Born on December 27, 1965, in Indore, India, he belongs to a family deeply rooted in Bollywood; his father, Salim Khan, is a celebrated screenwriter.
